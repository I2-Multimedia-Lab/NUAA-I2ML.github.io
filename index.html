INTRODUCTION

The Immersive and Interactive Multimedia Laboratory (I2ML) is affiliated with College of Computer Science and Technology/ College of Artificial Intelligence, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China. This lab conducts research in the areas of Image/Video Processing, Computer Vision, 3D Vision, Machine learning, etc.

Faculty Member

Pan Gao

I am currently an Associate Professor at the College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China. I received my PhD degree in Electronic Engineering from University of Southern Queensland, Toowoomba, Australia, luckily supervised by Prof. Wei Xiang and Prof. John Billingsley. During my PhD study, I spent two wonderful months as a visiting student at Charles Sturt University, Bathurst, Australia, working with Prof. Manoranjan Paul. From 2018 to 2019, I was fortunate enough to be a member of V-SENSE project, at Trinity College Dublin, Ireland, performing research in immersive multimedia processing under the supervision of Prof. Aljosa Smolic. My current research interests include Image Processing/Analysis, Deep Learning, Artificial Intelligence.
